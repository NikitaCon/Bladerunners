About Us

Kev's has been located on shopstreet, Galway for 7 years now and has become the most popular spot for students and tourists to relax and get a bite to eat. It has many couches and tables for people to eat and drink aswell a watching TV. Their is a wide variety of food and drink to eat their making it one of the popular places to eat on shopstreet.
